Re-inventing impact economy

The 1st impact payment and reward mobile app

Poi Pay is a mobile payment application that revitalizes local economies. It makes it possible to locate and pay in local currency the responsible tradesmen around you. These exchanges are reflected in a local impact gauge that certifies the quality of each user's consumption and rewards them accordingly for their positive impact on the territory.
